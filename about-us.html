BRIEF HISTORY OF SUBIM INTERNATIONAL LIMITED

SUBIM is an Engineering, Construction, Procurement, Installation and Maintenance (EPCIM) Company, formally incorporated with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) on the 21st of July 1992.

For over 18 years, SUBIMhas handled various projects and numerous business operations for a wide clientele base covering corporate institutions and individuals. To facilitate the identification, acquisition and transfer of new technologies to Nigeria, we have incorporated two holding companies abroad in Germany and U.S.A.  

WE are justifiably proud of having satisfied the demands of our esteemed clients; this to a very large extent can be traced to our competence and professionalism evident in the execution of every project and service we render; our respect for specifications, timely delivery, safe operations, technical innovations, and quality of our products.  

OUR VISSON


SUBIM aims at being the leading EPCIM Company in Africa while maintaining a safe, peaceful and infection free environment by ensuring the implementation of right company management and proactive policies.  

OUR MISSION


SUBIM will continue to commit itself to creating an indigenous outfit capable of providing Quality Service (QS) & Quality Management (QM) in the area of EPCIM using the latest technologies available & at competitive cost thereby building in our employees the confidence & integrity needed to work & satisfy all the requirements of our clients

OUR PERSONNEL

The overall activities of the company is run by a team of young, energetic, highly skilled and experienced professionals under the leadership of two co-founders whose profiles are below:

CHAIRMAN
Pastor S.O. Omoaluna

He is the Chairman, He is a graduate of Economics and Civil engineering from the University of Ife, now Obafemi Awolowo University in 1983 and 1987 respectively.

Managing Director/CEO
Pastor S.O. Omoaluna

Engr. (Mrs.) M.O.R Ikhalo is the managing director. She has a first degree in chemical engineering from the University of Timis, Roma in Europe and a Masters Degree in the same field from the university of Lagos, Nigeria. She is a registered professional engineer with the Council for the Regulation of Engineering in Nigeria (COREN), Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE) and Nigerian Society of Chemical Engineers (NSCh.E) with over 25 years of experience in the Oil & Gas industry, Energy as well as the construction industry.

Below are profiles of the General managers of our companies in Nigeria, U.S.A and Germany.

GENERAL MANAGER: (U.S.A)
Engr. Osezua Unoko

He is a graduate of Electrical Engineering from St. Paul’s University, Minnesota, U.S.A. He is a registered professional engineer with over 12 years’ experience in the industry.

GENERAL MANAGER:(NIGERIA).
Engr. Joseph Uwagbale

He is the general manager, He has a first degree in Civil Engineering and is a member of the British Safety Council. He has experiences in the Energy, Construction, Oil & Gas industry and a strong portfolio of achievements that includes fast track growth for start-up enterprises and significant quality and safety improvements.

GENERAL MANAGER: (GERMANY).
