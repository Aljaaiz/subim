WHAT WE DO

Procurement & Supply

Our company observes diligently its Quality policy to provide qualitative procurement and supply services to all our clients in the Oil & Gas, Energy and Construction industries respectively. We procure and supply a wide range of technical equipment, medical equipment and spare parts for oil field operations, refineries, petro-chemical plants, gas plant projects, construction operations, academic institutions, medical and laboratory facilities and many other industries. Chemical products, fertilizer and couplings are supplied based on established systems and procedures developed from the expertise and experience of our procurement personnel in accordance to the manufacturer’s specifications and in association with our local and foreign partners.

H - VAC Cooling Systems and Electro-mechanical Engineering

We render the following servicers to clients Supply, installation and maintenance of Window split unit and package (or stand-alone), central, H-VAC air conditioners, refrigerators and over hauling equipment such as generators, gas turbines / transformers and the supply of their spare parts.

Electrical controls and instrumentation
Fabrication, installation and commissioning of streetlights of various types.
Mechanical constructions, fabrication, erection and maintenance of air-condition ducting systems.
Mechanical construction, fabrication and installation of automatic control switches and distribution of electric control panels.

Water and Environmental Services

Our water and environmental operations have a reputation for meeting regulatory and legislative requirements associated with environmental assessment, project licensing, and remediation, protection and management services.

We render the following services;
Environmental evaluation report (water, soil & air), environmental base line studies and environmental impact assessment studies.
Design, construction and rehabilitation of waste system.
Construction and piping overhead water tank and tower
Supply of protector water treatment plant and installation of same.
Design and installation of sewage treatment plant.
Fabrication and rehabilitation
Water drilling project (Rig) and distribution.
Supply of O ten water sterilizers for use in lavation of agricultural/marine products, swimming pools, factories, schools, hotels, hospitals, homes, pharmaceutical/food processing industries, large water tanks and cooling towers.

Corrosion Management 

We initiate and handle various kinds of corrosion prevention and control programs and have developed procedures for selecting the best methods for appropriate remediation.

The major aspect of our corrosion management strategy includes:
Implementation of corrosion monitoring programs.
Pipeline pigging programs.
Coating specification and prevention.
Internal corrosion evaluation pipe – metal loss and cracks.
NDT and cathodic protection and corrosion control.
Hydrostatic pressure testing, etc.
